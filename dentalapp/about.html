About Us

Meet our dentists from who are part-time dentist part-time badass.

Benjamin Lewis Salomon

Benjamin Lewis Salomon is a graduate of the University of Southern California School of Dentistry, he then register himself as an army dentist but was replaced as a surgeon in his battalion after they were wounded.

Doc Holliday

John Henry "Doc" Holliday is an American gambler, gunfighter, and dentist. He is also a graduate from University of Pennsylvania Dental School. Holliday is best known for his role in the events leading up to and following the Gunfight at the O.K. Corral.

Robert Tanner Freeman

Robert Tanner Freeman is the first professionally trained black dentist in the United States. He eventually entered Harvard University and graduated as a dentist.
